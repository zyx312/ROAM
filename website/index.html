参考架构：三层决策模型Reference Architecture: Three-Layer Decision Model

第一层: AI自主响应Layer 1: AI Autonomous Response

70%

AI识别场景、评估风险、生成并执行响应方案。例如：安全靠边停车、绕行障碍物、重启感知模块。AI identifies scenario, assesses risk, generates and executes response plan. Examples: pull over safely, reroute, restart perception module.

第二层: AI辅助 + 人工确认Layer 2: AI-Assisted + Human Confirm

25%

AI生成带置信度的推荐方案，远程操作员审核确认后执行。例如：复杂路口脱困、交警手势识别。AI generates recommended action with confidence score. Remote operator reviews, confirms, then vehicle executes.

第三层: 远程驾驶 / 现场派遣Layer 3: Remote Driving / On-Site Dispatch

5%

≤10 km/h全遥控驾驶，或派遣现场救援。例如：车辆硬件故障、事故现场、车辆起火。Full teleoperation at ≤10 km/h, or dispatch on-site rescue. Examples: hardware failure, accident scene, vehicle fire.
